I started making music back in 2019 after getting inspired by artists like Aphex Twin, Venetian Snares and Squarepusher. Since then, music became one of the main ways for me to express my feelings.

I make music with genres like breakcore, maidcore, speedcore, ambient, DSBM and metal, also mixing elements from different styles together.


I don't like sticking to the same genre, and usually make experimental tracks that sound different from each other.

I started producing in FL Studio 12 and later switched to FL Studio 21. Over the years I've released more than 500 tracks across multiple projects and aliases. Not including released music, I have around 5,000 FL Studio projects.

One of my older projects was Noillox, a breakcore project active from 2019 to 2021. Back then I was also part of a small group with friends called "192" where we made breakcore music together. Most of those tracks are lost media now. Only a few tracks survived due to them being stored in Google Drive on one of my accounts.

A huge part of my inspiration comes from games i've played over the years. I usually try to reflect my current mood and the way the outside world feels to me through music.


In 2022, a lot of bad things happened, a lot of my irl friends have fled the country, leaving me feeling isolated.

Since then, I've started rapidly making songs in order to comfort myself with the harsh reality.

I'm basically a chronically online person who got into making music and games. I've worked on a lot of projects, even though most of them eventually ended up getting cancelled.

From a young age, I was drawn to making games because everything felt the same to me, and I wanted to create something new for myself.

I started making games on Roblox in late 2016, when I was around ██ years old. Most of them weren't good since I was just making them out of boredom.

Horror games were always something I wanted to make, mainly because I was a big fan of old poorly-made Unity and Game Jolt games.

Since I didn't know much about scripting back then, I relied on Toolbox assets for most of my projects back then.


In total, I've made over 2000 places on Roblox, all of which will remain unfinished.


Sometime around 2018, I moved to the Source Engine and started making maps for Half-Life 2 and Garry's Mod using the Hammer Editor.

Some of them were sandbox maps, while others were horror campaign maps where you had to escape from some god-knows-what place.


In 2019, I started making music in FL Studio 12.

My main genres were breakcore and ambient, inspired by artists like Venetian Snares and Aphex Twin.


I kept making Source Engine mods, music, and games until 2022.

That's when Parallix Studios was formed.

Parallix Studios was my Roblox game studio, consisting of a few close friends.


Our first project was FORGOTTEN, a singleplayer horror game inspired by Silent Hill and Resident Evil.


A few years later, in 2024, Parallix Studios was disbanded due to lack of developers and progress.
